Project idea


The project was built to be something that benefits humanity, and we decided what better way then to help predict when one of the most common and destructive storms. Hurricanes affect thousands of people every year, and every year our systems are improved to help those people gain an early start on they're preparations


Project description

This project was a simple weather forecasting application for the local weather that would also monitor atmospheric conditions to help predict if conditions are right where a hurricane can form based on previous storms. This will alert the user that they are in a vulnerable area and show them suggested routes to evacuate.
